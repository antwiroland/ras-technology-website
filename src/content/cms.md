# Retail Customer Tracking & Sales Rep Management System

## Project Overview
This platform provides **real-time visibility into sales operations**, combining customer mapping, sales rep tracking, route optimization, sales recording, and performance analytics.

## Problem Statement
- Limited visibility into field activities
- Difficulty verifying actual customer visits
- Poor route planning and wasted time
- Fragmented customer and sales data

## Solution
The system introduces a **map-driven field sales platform** that:
- Tracks sales rep locations and visits in real time
- Maps customers by vertical and geography
- Automatically logs visits using GPS and timestamps
- Optimizes daily routes for efficiency
- Generates actionable dashboards and reports

## Key Modules
- Organizational & user management
- Customer management with GPS
- Sales rep tracking & activity monitoring
- Route optimization & navigation
- Sales recording & proof of visit
- Dashboards & reporting
